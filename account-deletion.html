Account & data deletion

For the mobile app The Black Iron Gym, developed and offered by Arfen Fitness.

You may request deletion of your app account and associated personal data that we hold in connection with The Black Iron Gym. This page explains how to submit that request.

How to request deletion

Send an email from the address or phone number associated with your membership (if applicable) so we can verify your identity.

Email

markagedigital00@gmail.com

Use subject line: Account deletion — The Black Iron Gym

In your message, include:

  • Your full name
  • The mobile number used to sign in to the app (for member accounts), or the email used for staff/admin accounts
  • Gym or location name if your account is tied to a specific gym
  • A clear statement that you want your account and associated personal data deleted

What we delete

After we confirm your request, we will delete or anonymize your account and associated personal data held for The Black Iron Gym services, except where we must retain certain information to meet legal, tax, or fraud-prevention obligations. We will confirm by email when processing is complete or if we need more information.

Timing

We aim to complete verified deletion requests within thirty (30) days.

Questions

For privacy questions, see our privacy policy or contact us at the same support email above, referencing The Black Iron Gym.
